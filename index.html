Analysis Types

Publication-Grade Analysis

RMSD Analysis

Measures the average deviation of atomic positions over time, indicating system stability and convergence. Includes backbone, C-alpha, and all-atom RMSD plots.

Backbone RMSD Cα RMSD All-atom

RMSF Analysis

Per-residue fluctuation analysis identifying flexible loops, active site residues, and stable core regions of the protein structure during simulation.

Per-residue B-factor Flexibility map

SASA Analysis

Solvent-accessible surface area tracking to monitor protein folding/unfolding events, hydrophobic core exposure, and ligand binding surface changes.

Total SASA Hydrophobic Hydrophilic

Distance Analysis

Time-resolved distance measurements between selected atoms, residues, or domains to track conformational transitions and binding pocket dynamics.

Pairwise COM distance Time-series

Atom-Contact Analysis

Comprehensive contact map analysis showing persistent inter-atomic contacts, hydrogen bond occupancy, and salt bridge formation throughout the simulation.

H-bonds Salt bridges Contact map

Trajectory & Visualization

Clustered trajectory snapshots and optional PyMOL/VMD session files for visual inspection and presentation-quality structural figures.

Clustering PyMOL session Snapshots
