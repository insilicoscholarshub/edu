RMSD Analysis

Measures the average deviation of atomic positions over time, indicating system stability and convergence. Essential for validating that your simulation has reached equilibrium before extracting meaningful data.

Backbone RMSD Cα RMSD All-atom RMSD Ligand RMSD

RMSF Analysis

Per-residue fluctuation analysis identifying flexible loops, active site residues, and stable core regions. RMSF correlates with B-factors from X-ray crystallography, enabling direct validation of your simulation against experimental data.
